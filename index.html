Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you're exploring performance guarantees, bond structuring, or capital advisory services, it's important to have clarity before engaging. We've compiled answers to the most common questions our clients ask—covering everything from turnaround times to the types of institutions we serve.

What types of guarantees do you provide?

We specialize in structuring performance guarantees, bid bonds, advance payment guarantees, and custom financial instruments tailored for high-value contracts in infrastructure, construction, and public procurement. These are issued through trusted financial institutions or insurance partners.

Do you only work with large institutions?

While we focus on governments, parastatals, banks, and corporates, we also support mid-sized private firms that need professionally structured guarantees or capital solutions for major tenders or investment deals.

Can you help with bond issuance in Kenya?

Yes. We provide end-to-end bond advisory, including MTN programs, sovereign or county-linked infrastructure bonds, and regulatory support through the Capital Markets Authority (CMA). We also assist with rating agency engagement and investor targeting.

How fast can I get a performance guarantee?

Depending on the complexity and required backing, we typically structure and deliver clean, compliant guarantees within 3–5 business days, subject to documentation and due diligence.

Do you offer support for public-private partnerships (PPPs)?

Absolutely. We advise both government entities and private developers on how to structure bankable PPPs, including risk allocation, project viability, and capital mobilization through blended finance or institutional investors.
